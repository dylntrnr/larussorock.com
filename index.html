Larusso

Combining smart hooks and catchy lyrics Larusso delivers high powered music for everyone.

Larusso has recorded five independently funded albums, with the newest being 2012’s ‘Tell Me Everything’ EP produced by Allen Combs (Search/Rescue) and recorded at Rival Gray Studios in Everett, WA. Larusso has become one of Salt Lake City’s premier acts and has amassed a loyal local and national fan base. Larusso has sucessfully toured the western United States since 2006. Other notable achievements include being selected by Alternative Press Magazine as a finalist in the 2006 ‘Almost Famous’ contest and in 2008 winning the regional Ernie Ball ‘Battle of the Bands’ and performing at the Vans Warped Tour.
