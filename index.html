Larusso

Combining smart hooks and catchy lyrics Larusso delivers high powered music for everyone.

Larusso is Salt Lake City's premier rock artist and has amassed a loyal following both at home and nationally. Through relentless marketing and promotion, and several years of independently funded national touring campaigns, Larusso has become the quintessential DIY band, and evolved from your run-of-the-mill local artist to a seasoned rock outfit.

They have won fans over with the high-energy modern rock sound of artists like Anberlin and Brand New, and the classic pop-punk feeling of Blink-182 and Yellowcard, infused with smart hooks and grabbing lyrical work. Larusso's unique twist on their sound, combined with an intense and engaging live performance has created a constantly increasing fan base of all ages.

In addition to national touring, Larusso has had the opportunity to share the stage with such artists as The Almost, Go Radio, Transit, and Cartel, as well as headlining local festivals and events with several thousand in attendance and playing on the Ernie Ball stage at Warped Tour. Larusso has independently released an extensive catalog of EP's and LP's, the latest of which is 2014's "Life In Static", slated for release 8/16/14.

Larusso is Nick, Aaron, Fizzy, and Justin, coming from a wide variety of musical backgrounds and projects to create a unique, yet cohesive take on alternative rock and pop punk
